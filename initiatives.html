Initiatives



Speakers/AMAs

Our community Slack is a central platform for students to discuss, connect, and post about opportunities within the social impact space. We also update our members through our weekly announcements.

Community Slack

We host monthly fireside chats with social impact speakers for students to engage and better understand how they can land roles for social good. We welcome speakers of all backgrounds and industries!

Resource Hub

The Impact Fellows Resource Hub is a database full of articles, content and continuously updated job postings. Impact Fellows members will receive exclusive access to these materials.

Coffee Chats

Students can connect with peers and professionals to have one-on-one conversations on the future of social innovation and how they can address social issues within their local communities.

Mentors

By creating an incredible community of peers and professionals, we help students find personalized mentors to advance their social impact careers and make meaningful connections within their respective fields.

Interview Prep

Purusing a career in social impact involves balancing the head with the heart. We help students understand their “why” when interviewing by hosting workshops and providing resource guides to navigate these interviews.
